TRANSACTION INFO & TIPS

Get to know more for easy and safety transaction for your convenience. There are more TRANSACTIONS on IStakeNBTC Platform to understand, they are:

  • ACTIVATE – OPTION A

    • is to stake or to stack NBTC BOUNTY 30% + ACTIVATION BALANCE 30% + NBTC LIQUID 40% getting moved to FROZEN / STAKING POOL by choosing staking package amount.

  • ACTIVATE – OPTION B

    • is to stake or stack NBTC BOUNTY 30% + ACTIVATION BALANCE 70% getting moved to FROZEN / STAKING POOL by choosing staking package amount.

  • SEND BOUNTY

    • is for transferring BOUNTY BALANCE to others staking account internally within the same line generation by using userid. For functioning must create NBTC BOUNTY address first.

  • SEND INTERNAL

    • is P2P transaction for transferring NBTC LIQUID BALANCE to internal NBTC Wallet Address within staking platform to the same line generation by using NBTC Wallet Address. Blockchain fee applied 0.0001 NBTC every transaction.

  • SEND EXTERNAL

    • is for transferring NBTC LIQUID BALANCE to external wallet, like: Exchanger Wallet, DApps Wallet, NBTC Payment Gateway or others NBTC External Wallet platform. Send External Fee 10% applied and one time limitation every 30 days with max. 25% from staking package.

  • RECEIVE

    • is used to receive your NBTC from any Liquid NBTC both internal nor external wallet & also from Bounty Wallet / Platform.

  • WITHDRAW REWARD

    • is for claiming or collecting your Liquid NBTC as rewards from NeoBitcoin PoS MINING POOL to be topped up to your NBTC LIQUID BALANCE.

  • CLAIM FROZEN - STOP

    • is for claiming or collecting your NBTC FROZEN after Staking Contract or Period fully accomplished from PoS MINING POOL.

  • REFRESH TO LIQUID

    • is for moving your ACTIVATION BALANCE to NBTC LIQUID BALANCE.
